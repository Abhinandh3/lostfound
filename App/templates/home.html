The Process

How LostFind Works

A secure, streamlined system designed to take the panic out of losing things and make returning items effortless.

01

File a Report

Submit detailed information about the item. Upload photos, pinpoint the location on our map, and provide unique identifiers to ensure accuracy.

02

Smart Matching

Our algorithm instantly cross-references your submission with our community database, filtering by time, location radius, and item category.

03

Secure Return

Once a match is verified, we facilitate a secure, anonymous connection between the finder and owner to arrange a safe handover.
